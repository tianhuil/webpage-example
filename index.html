Who We Are

Our mission is to organize and support people across America as they advocate to protect our liberal, tolerant values, and to help the middle and working class. Our three main goals are 1.) to fight back against media complacency, especially in their false equivalency and failure to report Trump's scandals; 2.) to contact our elected representatives and encourage them to respect the centuries-old democratic values of America; and 3.) to support other organizations in their diverse ways of opposing normalization.

Together, we can continue the energy poured out over the past few days. We can show the world that America IS already a good place, and with our work America Will Be a better place tomorrow for all Americans (as the Langston Hughes poem from which we take our name promises.) This belief -- this optimism -- isn't easy, but we will work hard to make it a reality.
